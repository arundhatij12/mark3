BTS : Seven Angels Ruling The World

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy bandthat began formation in 2010 and debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. The septet—composed of Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin,V, and Jungkook —co-writes and co-produces much of their own output. Kudos to the setpet.
